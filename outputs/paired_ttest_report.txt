Paired analysis-time comparison
========================================
Number of paired scenario runs: 20

Configurations:
A = standalone Cowrie / manual log analysis
B = distributed honeynet / parser-assisted normalized analysis

Descriptive statistics:
Mean time, Configuration A: 14.80 minutes
Mean time, Configuration B: 6.10 minutes
Mean paired difference A - B: 8.70 minutes
SD of paired differences: 3.59
SE of paired differences: 0.80

Paired t-test:
t(19) = 10.84
p < 0.001
95% CI for mean difference: [7.02, 10.38] minutes
Effect size, Cohen's dz: 2.42

Suggested manuscript sentence:
The mean manual analysis time was 14.80 minutes, whereas parser-assisted normalized analysis required 6.10 minutes on average. The mean paired reduction was 8.70 minutes (SD = 3.59). A paired t-test confirmed a statistically significant reduction, t(19) = 10.84, p < 0.001.
